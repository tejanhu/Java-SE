Scenario:
Traveller explores Sahara Desert, lost on his own trying to find a way out, he/she only has access to a compass that directs him/her how close he/she is to finding the exit. 
He/She can go "E", "N","W" or "S" i.e.: East, North, West, or South respectively, and depending where he/she goes they will be given a distance reading how close or far they are to the (exit) riverbanks in metres.
